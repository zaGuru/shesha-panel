Shesha D Is The Best Delivery Service Near You. You Order From Your Phone We Take It To Your Door Step. It's never been easier.

Consumer App

With the consumer app customers will be able to make an order , go to checkout and track their order. The application consists of many functions including :

Being able to view their favorite foods, check out highly rated stores and their menus, browsing through different local cusines, attaching their location for the most accurate delivery point. And how can we forget our safe and secure online payment gateway.

It's a very modern yet simple food delivery app that will give our users the best food delivery system. Check it out now by simply downloading the app on the home page.

Driver App

The driver app is used by our drivers who have gone through the vetting process and were successful. The application consists of many functions including :

Being able to view incoming order requests, tracking orders that you have made and balance that is due to you, able to navigate to the restaurant and the customer using google maps.

We pride ourselves in making sure that consumers have the best experience therefore we make sure to have the best and professional drivers to help us achive that. Click join us now and become one of our drivers.

Restaurant App

The restaurant app is used by our restaurant owners and employees. The application consists of many functions including :

Being able to track incoming orders, being able to keep track of each order simultaneously, being able to manage orders and your products using the app and also keeping track of all orders that have made and balance the balance that is due to you.

Our restaurant are also a big part of our system as consumers are always looking for new local cuisines to try out. Click Join us now and start the jorney of becoming one of our registered restaurants.

Please note that only restaurant and drivers who have gone through our in-depth vetting process will be able to sign in on the driver and restaurant app.
